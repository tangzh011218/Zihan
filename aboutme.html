About Me

An illustration of the fight against COVID-19

"Talented,

  brilliant, amazing,

  show stopping, spectacular, never the same,

  totally unique, completely not ever been done before,

  put it in a blender, shit on it, vomit on it, eat it, give birth to it."

I'm Zihan Tang, a graphic designer and illustrator currently based in Boston, USA.

Growing up, I watched countless animations and was always in awe of beautifully illustrated books, which ultimately paved the way for me to become a designer and illustrator. When I'm away from my laptop, I'm busy being a book freak, a movie buff, a video game fanatic, hanging out and enjoying the outdoors as much as possible.
